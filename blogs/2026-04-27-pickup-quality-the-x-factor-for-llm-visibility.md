When distributing content, placement quality is all that matters When considering content distribution solutions, many brands prioritize the number of pickups or placements of a piece over the quality of the domains distributed to. However, our study of 215 stories and 54,702 AI responses showed one very consistent trend: it’s the quality of a distributed placement that matters most.
